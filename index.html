Who are we?
Our organisation was formed by a group of students and young working people, with the aim of providing authentic information, and to connect those who have resources to those who need them. We're also working to collaborate with the NGO Bhumi to expand our operations and reach, ensuring life-saving COVID resources reach the people that require them, as fast as possible.
